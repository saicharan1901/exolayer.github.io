Our Story

Specialized Engineering & Technology.

EXOLAYER Private Limited, based in Bangalore, is a registered Indian private company incorporated in Dec 2025. We are a core team of 5 specialists focused on complex electromechanical integrations, AI & ML, advanced structural design, mission-critical system development, and additive & composite parts manufacturing for the aerospace, defence, and automotive sectors.

At EXOLAYER, we bridge the gap between cutting-edge digital innovation and robust physical manufacturing. By bringing mechanical design, electronics, software, and advanced materials under one roof, we eliminate the traditional silos that slow down product development. Our mission is to provide the "Digital Backbone" for physical production, enabling rapid innovation cycles that were previously impossible for complex industrial projects.

Whether it's designing a natural fibre composite component, building an AI-powered manufacturing marketplace, or engineering mission-critical defence equipment, our team is driven by precision and scalability. We partner closely with our clients — taking their core problem statements and delivering turnkey, production-ready solutions.
